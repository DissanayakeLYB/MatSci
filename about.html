Hey there!

My name is Lasith Dissanayake, and I am passionate about Materials Science and Engineering. With a background in Metallurgy, Smart Materials, Photovoltaic Materials, and Polymers, I aim to share my knowledge and insights with a wider audience.

I also have experience in 3D modeling and simulations, using software such as SolidWorks, SolidEdge, AutoCAD, and Blender for 3D animations. My experience includes handling complex projects and developing innovative solutions in the field of materials science.

Through this website, I hope to provide valuable information, articles, and resources that will inspire and educate those interested in this fascinating field. Whether you are a student, researcher, or enthusiast, I invite you to explore the content and join me on this journey of discovery.
